About Us

The Coding Club, IIT Guwahati, is a vibrant community dedicated to helping students discover and nurture their passion for programming. As part of the Institute Technical Council, we provide mentorship, organize events, and foster a collaborative environment for all coders, from beginners to experts.

Our mission is to create a platform where students can learn, build, and grow together. Whether you're interested in competitive programming, open-source, or building real-world projects, there's a place for you here!
