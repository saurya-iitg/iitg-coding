Our Initiatives

  • Internship Preparation Program: DSA booklets, tech sessions, and alumni mentorship to help students ace their internship season.
  • Wiki Portal: A curated collection of tutorials and resources, written by IITG students, to help beginners get started with programming.
  • Learner's Space: Summer courses and weekly assignments on topics like Python, Machine Learning, and Web Development.
  • Seasons of Code: A summer-long open-source project program inspired by GSoC, with mentorship from seniors and alumni.
